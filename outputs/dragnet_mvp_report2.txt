# DRAGNET Report: Trump Dementia

## Fact-Focused Summary

The topic of President Donald Trump's mental health, particularly regarding claims of dementia, has been a subject of public discussion and speculation. Various public figures, including actress Rosie O'Donnell and Illinois Governor JB Pritzker, have made statements suggesting that Trump exhibits signs of dementia. O'Donnell predicted that Trump "won't survive that long" in office due to visible dementia, while Pritzker has also expressed concerns about Trump's cognitive health, citing changes in his speech and behavior. However, a former aide, Anthony Scaramucci, has stated that while Trump is "genuinely unwell," claims of dementia are unfounded. The White House has rejected claims regarding Trump's health, asserting that it remains "exceptional."
