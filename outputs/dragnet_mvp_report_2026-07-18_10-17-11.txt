# DRAGNET Report on Claims of Trump's Health and Dementia

## Fact-Focused Summary

Recent discussions surrounding President Donald Trump's health have included claims of dementia, primarily stemming from public comments made by various individuals, including celebrities and politicians. Notably, Rosie O'Donnell has predicted that Trump "won't survive" his term due to alleged dementia, while Illinois Governor JB Pritzker has also suggested that Trump is showing signs of dementia based on observed changes in his speech and behavior. However, a former aide, Anthony Scaramucci, has stated that while Trump is "genuinely unwell," claims of dementia are unfounded. The White House has rejected claims regarding Trump's health, asserting that it remains "exceptional."
