# DRAGNET Report on Claims of Trump's Health and Dementia

## Fact-Focused Summary

Recent discussions surrounding former President Donald Trump's health have included claims of dementia, primarily stemming from public comments made by various individuals, including actress Rosie O'Donnell and Illinois Governor JB Pritzker. O'Donnell has suggested that Trump "won't survive" his term due to dementia, while Pritzker has also expressed concerns about Trump's cognitive state, citing changes in his speech and behavior. However, some sources, including a former aide, have disputed the claim that Trump has dementia, stating that while he may be "genuinely unwell," the specific diagnosis of dementia is unfounded. The White House has rejected claims regarding Trump's health, asserting that it remains "exceptional."
